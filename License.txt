Installation
=============

You definitely need LAM/MPI, and some basic setup for the web-based
setting up of LAM, etc. See the Asterias-common set of files, also
available from The Launchpad (https://launchpad.net/projects/asterias) or
Bioinformatics.org (http://bioinformatics.org/asterias).
